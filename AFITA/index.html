Scope

Risk register

Risks & dependencies

What can derail this project — and how we mitigate. Severity reflects probability × impact on Aug 10 release.
AFITA · KICKOFF
ChatGPT Enterprise data may not be fully automatable
High
Impact ChatGPT metrics may be limited to aggregated views, with details adjusted after Discovery.
Mitigation Validate during Discovery Week. Prioritize metrics we can obtain reliably.
Microsoft 365 admin permissions delayed
High
Impact Data connection work blocked. Delays cascade into Sprint 2.
Mitigation Access checklist closed at kickoff with named AFITA owners and deadlines.
Department info in Microsoft 365 may be incomplete
Medium
Impact Some users will appear without a department. Data quality affected.
Mitigation Validate during Discovery Week. Surface "no department" users clearly in the dashboard.
Confusion: this is not a productivity, ROI, or audit tool
High
Impact Expanded scope, privacy risks, missed release date.
Mitigation Keep the agreed scope. Excluded items go to a future phase.
Jul 23 demo is outside the original proposal scope
High
Impact Original proposal includes only screen designs + a dev version — neither suitable for Seattle executives. Time during Discovery Week partially used for this.
Mitigation Formal adjustment using Sec. 13 of the proposal. AFITA sign-off required by Jun 26. Demo absorbed in existing commercial scope.
Source data may be delayed or refreshed on a schedule
Medium
Impact Dashboard does not show real-time activity; potential stakeholder confusion.
Mitigation "Last refreshed" indicators in the dashboard. Set expectations with leadership upfront.
Data connection failures without proactive monitoring
Medium
Impact Silent data quality issues. If a data connection fails (Microsoft 365, Copilot, or ChatGPT Enterprise), no one notices until someone spots strange numbers. Executives could be acting on stale data.
Mitigation First version includes a basic data status screen showing last update time for each connection (per proposal Sec. 5) — visual review only, no automatic alerts. Assign AFITA owner during kickoff to define a check-in cadence. Proactive alerts and automatic recovery move to a future phase.
Security review takes longer than the timeline
Medium
Impact Production launch in Sprint 3 delayed past Aug 10.
Mitigation Start security validation in Sprint 1. First version handles only adoption indicators, no sensitive content.
AFITA stakeholder availability for reviews
Low
Impact Design reviews and validation testing delayed; decisions get stuck.
Mitigation Weekly cadence locked. Async review windows in Basecamp.
